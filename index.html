The Machine

The Car

1993 Volvo 940

A racing icon meticulously engineered by Carl Jardevall, the legendary "Sideways Swede." This remarkable vehicle has been driven to numerous stage victories by Nate Tennis, showcasing its enduring prowess and precision. Now under the command of Alexander, the Volvo 940 competes in the open 2WD class, where its unassuming 4-cylinder turbo engine and 4-speed dogbox work in harmony to blend classic elegance with competitive edge. A testament to its creators' vision and the ongoing dedication of its drivers, this Volvo continues to redefine the boundaries of motorsport excellence.

The Crew

Meet Our Team

Alex Jardevall
Driver
Bay Area, CA

Alexander is a Registered Nurse with a diverse background in dodging the chaos of San Francisco's traffic as an EMT driving an ambulance to the limelight as an actor and model. With rally racing in his blood, thanks to his father, the legendary "Sideways Swede," he channels the coolness of Steve McQueen and the sophistication of Paul Newman, aiming to blend high-octane thrills with a dash of silver-screen flair. Adrenaline and charm are his trademarks, and he's always geared up for the next adventure—whether it's on the racetrack or the runway.

Max Melcher
Co-Driver
Houston, TX

Max is a versatile professional who balances his career in tech with his passion for racing. As a Product Manager at a blockchain company, he helps developers create cutting-edge decentralized applications. Outside of work, Max is a commercial-rated pilot and certified EMT, skills that complement his role as a co-driver. Max's journey into rally racing began when he met Alex during his EMT training. When not in the co-driver's seat, Max channels his energy into training for triathlons. He also enjoys expanding his knowledge through podcasts and traveling.

Ridge Rally

Kern County, California

The Ridge Rally features 141+ stage miles of open, flat-out roads that transition into switchbacks and hairpins, all while traversing a 4,000 ft elevation change. These stage miles make The Ridge one of the longest events on the ARA calendar. An ARA Western Regional Championship event run on gravel through the mountains of Southern California.

Olympus Rally Upcoming

Shelton, Washington

The Olympus Rally is one of the oldest and most iconic rallies in the United States, with roots dating back to 1968. Hosted outside Shelton, WA, its 140 stage miles make up some of the longest stages of the ARA season, with some clocking in at over 20 miles. A true test of endurance and speed through the forests of the Pacific Northwest.

Tour de Forest

Shelton, Washington

The Tour de Forest Rally traces its roots back to the late 1970s, first organized by the Rainier Auto Sports Club. This compact, one-day ARA West Region Championship event covers 34.6 stage miles across four special stages. Tour de Forest's schedule is unique in that it combines recce and competition on the same day, sending teams through the tree-covered roads south of Seattle.
